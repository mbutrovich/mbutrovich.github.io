I am a recent Ph.D. graduate (May 2024) from the [Computer Science Department](https://www.csd.cs.cmu.edu), [School of Computer Science](https://www.cs.cmu.edu), at [Carnegie Mellon University](https://www.cmu.edu), researching database management systems (DBMSs). I explored operating system and DBMS codesign opportunities via safe kernel extension mechanisms like [eBPF](https://ebpf.io/what-is-ebpf/). I was incredibly fortunate to be advised by [Andy Pavlo](http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~pavlo/), and I was a member of the [Database Group (CMU-DB)](https://db.cs.cmu.edu), [Parallel Data Lab (PDL)](https://www.pdl.cmu.edu/), and [Systems, Networking, and Performance (SNAP) Lab](https://snap.cs.cmu.edu).

I previously completed an M.Sc. in Computer Science at CMU, graduating in December 2018. Before graduate school, I worked at [Western Digital](https://www.westerndigital.com) for over eight years, performing firmware validation on storage devices while earning a B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering at [UC Irvine](https://uci.edu) and [Irvine Valley College](https://www.ivc.edu).

I am currently discussing with teams about industry roles. If you work on DBMS internals and think I might be a good fit, please see my résumé above and do reach out. I would love to chat with you!
